What this is

Leadership the way it actually shows up.

The Hard Way is a conversation between an operator and a coach about the principles leaders actually live by — and the moments that taught them.

Every episode takes three principles, and we each bring a real moment when one of them stopped being a line on a wall and started costing us something. No theory. No leadership-talk. Just the bruise and what it taught us.

How it works

A spine you can feel by minute eight.

The show is built around one question, asked of every guest, every episode. The format is a repeatable beat called The Principle Pass — same five steps every time, so listeners settle into the rhythm and we keep our promise to ship something dense in thirty minutes.

"What did you have to learn the hard way?"

0–1 min Read it. One of us reads the principle aloud, in full.
1–4 min The moment. A real story — the time this principle stopped being abstract.
4–6 min The other beat. The same principle, different lens. Operator vs. coach.
6–7 min The friction. Where we see it differently. What we'd edit.
~7 min The reframe. One sentence: how we'd word it differently now.
